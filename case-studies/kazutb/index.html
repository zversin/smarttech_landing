KazUTB Campus Integration Program

Complex modernization project across multiple university buildings with retained legacy assets, biometric expansion, and integrated attendance reporting.

Context

KazUTB required a modernization program for access and attendance operations without disrupting active campus traffic or fully replacing existing hardware estates.

Challenge

  • Retain and extend existing turnstile/control infrastructure where feasible.
  • Integrate additional face recognition terminals at key throughput points.
  • Connect attendance events to academic schedules and reporting requirements.
  • Deliver operational continuity during phased rollout.

Solution

  • Designed phased architecture combining existing controllers with new biometric terminals.
  • Implemented synchronization and quality checks for identity and event data.
  • Integrated schedule-based rules for attendance analytics and management reporting.
  • Established role-based operational dashboards and exception workflows.

Implementation

  • Stage 1: Baseline audit and interface mapping.
  • Stage 2: Hardware rollout and controller policy alignment.
  • Stage 3: Data migration and connector deployment.
  • Stage 4: Validation, handover, and support playbooks.
