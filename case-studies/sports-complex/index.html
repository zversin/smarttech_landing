Sports Complex Rollout

New facility launch with access control deployment, migration of legacy identity data, and attendance tracking readiness from day one.

Context

A new sports complex required rapid go-live for controlled entry and attendance tracking while preserving historical identity assets from a legacy environment.

Challenge

  • Launch a new access contour under tight timeline constraints.
  • Migrate photos and user records from legacy Perco Web to a new management environment.
  • Avoid manual re-enrollment workload before opening.
  • Provide reliable attendance visibility for operations from day one.

Solution

  • Deployed access control architecture for the sports complex entry zones.
  • Executed staged identity migration with validation checks for user and photo mapping.
  • Configured role-based rights and attendance event handling.
  • Prepared operational views for early-stage administration and support teams.

Implementation

  • Stage 1: Facility and hardware baseline assessment.
  • Stage 2: Controller and terminal commissioning.
  • Stage 3: Legacy data migration and reconciliation.
  • Stage 4: Go-live validation and operational handover.
