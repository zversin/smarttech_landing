About SmartTech

SmartTech is an engineering-driven systems integration company focused on access control, face recognition, and operational data integration.

Who we are

We deliver end-to-end integration projects where hardware, software, and data flows must work as one reliable system. Our teams cover architecture, field integration, migration, and operational support.

What we value

Engineering rigor

Design decisions are traceable, testable, and operationally grounded.

Reliability

Stable operations matter more than feature noise or unnecessary complexity.

Security-minded delivery

Access policies, auditability, and data handling practices are built in from day one.

Accountability

Clear ownership from discovery to support, including integration outcomes.

How we work

  • Technical discovery workshop and baseline risk assessment.
  • Architecture and staged rollout plan aligned to operations.
  • Deployment, integration, and validation with measurable acceptance criteria.
  • Handover, support model, and controlled change management.

Regions and delivery model

SmartTech is based in Kazakhstan and delivers projects for organizations requiring practical, engineering-led integration across campus and enterprise environments.
