Face Recognition Integration

Operationally reliable face recognition deployments integrated with access control, identity sources, and attendance policies.

Scope of delivery

  • Terminal selection guidance based on environment, throughput, and policy constraints.
  • Identity enrollment workflow design for staff, students, contractors, and visitors.
  • Integration with controller logic and fallback methods (cards, PIN, guarded entry).
  • Monitoring and quality checks for enrollment consistency and event reliability.

Implementation principles

Policy-first design

Biometric flows mapped to business rules, legal requirements, and access segmentation.

False acceptance considerations

Threshold tuning and exception paths handled as part of operational governance.

Enrollment governance

Identity quality, consent handling, and recertification procedures aligned to risk profile.

Operational resilience

Fallback paths and service continuity planning for high-traffic points of entry.

Where this solution fits

  • Campuses with schedule-linked attendance and controlled access zones.
  • Enterprise offices and plants requiring role-based identity verification.
  • Facilities that must modernize access without replacing all existing infrastructure.
